Welcome to Stevens Institute of Technology

Founded in 1870, Stevens is a top research university in Hoboken, NJ with a focus on technology and innovation.

Programs & Majors

  • Engineering & Technology
  • Computer Science
  • Business & Management
  • Sciences & Mathematics

SAT & ACT

Stevens is test-optional, but strong scores can strengthen your application.

Typical range: SAT 1330–1480, ACT 30–34

Tuition & Financial Aid

Tuition: Around $60,000/year before aid.

Stevens offers generous merit scholarships and need-based aid.
